People move and shift across devices and platforms throughout their day. From laptops to phones, from PCs to holograms. From office to living room, from real to virtual. Experiences seamlessly move and shift with them.

When design is intuitive, people are inspired to move forward. They’re understood and their behavior is anticipated. Experiences adapt to their needs, intent, and context.
